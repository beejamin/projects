MACS

Who doesn’t love cheesy goodness? Terrorists, that’s who. Macs is doing everything in their power to defeat the terrorists by creating an awesome menu consisting of a variety of mac n cheese. With such delicacies as mama’s mac, baked potato mac, and hangover mac, you really can’t go wrong at Macs. Each order is served in a cast iron skillet, because they keep it classy.

What to expect:

I think the main thing you can expect from a place called Macs is mac n cheese, and lots of it. They offer mac n cheese done 18 different ways, with some really unique options. Finishing the mac n cheese in a cast iron skillet adds some nice crispiness to the top of the mac n cheese as well as helps keep the dish hot and melty while you’re eating it. Plus, as a picky eater, Macs is always accommodating to removing something out of the order (I always get rid of the sour cream and chives). When in Rome, do as the Romans do… When in Wisconsin, do as the Wisconsinites do and eat cheese.


Do yourself a favor, go check out Macs next time you're in Green Bay and carb up on some great macaroni and cheese.

Rating: 4.5/5
