Legend Larry's

If you're a big wing person like I am, you have to check out Legend Larry's. In my opinion, Legend Larry's is the best wing joint in Green Bay. Their award winning traditional wings are nicely sized and go well with their signature sauces. Pair that with the fact that it's also a bar, and you have a great combination. You can drink and eat wings all night. If that's not a great time, I don't know what is.

What to expect:

When you sit down, you won't be greeted by wait staff, they don't have any. On each table there is some order forms and menus. You'll fill out the order form and give it to the bartender to place your order. You'll place your drink order while ordering your food as well. If you need a refill on your beverage, you head back up and order a refill.


Another thing to know about Legend Larry's, their menu is pretty limited. Aside from wings, they sell battered shrimp and appetizers. I'd say, if you're going to Legend Larry's, go for their award winning wings. Let's be honest, if you go to Legend Larry's and don't get their wings, you're truly missing out in your life.


*Disclaimer I'd be careful to go to Legend Larry's during a sporting event. I used to go there for the NFL Draft every year, but apparently their jukebox overrides their speaker system. During the draft we had to listen to one hit wonders because some hipsters loaded up the jukebox with them. We ended up having to leave to finish the draft at another location.*


My go-to order: 12 DOA traditional wings

Rating: 4.75/5
