The Green Bay Narrative

Episode 010 | Curt C.

On this episode we sit down with a California native and die-hard Packers fan - Curt. Curt and I have quite an extensive history and have known each other for quite some time. Even with me knowing Curt for a while, I was pleased to hear some new/unique stories that Curt had never shared with me. In this episode, we discuss how Curt became a Packers fan, how he got to know players, Packers training camp, and so much more. I think you'll really enjoy Curt's story as much as I did.
