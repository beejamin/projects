The Green Bay Narrative

Episode 001 | Wade S.

In the very first ever episode of The Green Bay Narrative, Wade and I sit down together so we can hear his story. Born and molded in the snow of the Upper Peninsula, Wade became a die-hard Packer fan at a very early age. In this episode, we’ll find out how Wade became a fan of the Green Bay Packers living in Michigan, what a typical game day was like for Wade in the UP, who is Wade’s favorite player, and much, much more. Wade has some great stories that you’re not going to want to miss!
