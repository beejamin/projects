Tapped Sports Bar & Grill

Tapped Sports Bar & Grill is located on the outskirts of Green Bay in Suamico. Tapped Sports Bar & Grill is not only a restaurant but also has a bowling alley attached to it. It's a great spot if you're looking to get dinner and kill some time by bowling a few frames.

What to expect:

Tapped Sports Bar and Grill isn't anything too flashy. It's your typical sports bar. It's one of those spots you'd gladly watch any game at with their multiple TVs, large bar, and vast menu.


When going over the menu, the Mac Attack caught my attention. The Mac Attack is a burger topped with house mac-n-cheese and bacon. I'll be honest with you...if I see that combo on a burger, I'm probably going to try it every time.


The food itself was good. I wouldn't call it great. It felt like it was lacking some flavor. My girlfriend tried the nachos and it didn't seem like her nachos had the same issue. So I'm guessing it could just be hit or miss.


The nice perk about this spot is that if you're looking to do something after dinner, you can walk into the back of the space and go bowling. They offer bowling by the hour, which is a different style of bowling because you can be in the middle of a game and have your time be up. I guess it helps if you're on a time schedule?


Overall, I'd gladly eat at Tapped Sports Bar again, but it wouldn't be my go-to spot to get something to eat. If I were looking to grab something to eat prior to bowling, I'd be happy to dine there though.

Rating: 3.9/5
