About

Me.

Hi, my name is Ben Biederman.

I'm currently a Client Relationship Manager along with Social Media Manager for SFI, which is a subsidary of Schneider National. I've had quite the unique path that has lead me to this role, with a background in business management, manufacturing, and human resources.

As a Client Relationship Manager, I am responsible for assessing the risk of a prospect; determining ways to mitigate any risk that may be present; bringing in new clients; building a strong relationship with those clients and assisting them in their success as a business owner.

When I'm not working, you’ll find me working on my side hustle, cheering on the Packers, drinking an unhealthy amount of coffee, skateboarding, or trying to learn something new.

Thanks for stopping by to visit my website. If you have any questions or you'd like to contact me, please feel free to shoot me an email.
