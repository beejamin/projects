Four-Way Bar

Located in Suamico, and well known for their Friday fish fry is Four-Way Bar & Restaurant. Four-Way Bar & Restaurant is a bar and restaurant (shocker by the name) that has been family owned since 1978, offering a wide-variety of dish options, it offers something for just about anyone.

What to expect:

Well, when it comes to what to expect, the first thing I'd say is a cluster. I entered the very busy location and walked to the hostess desk. After standing there for a five minutes or so, someone asked if they could help me. I stated that I was interesting in a table. I was advised that I had to speak with the bartenders to get a number, the number would then be the order in which I would be served. I headed over to the very bustling bar to get assigned a number. After waiting a few minutes to get my number, I found a small table to sit at in the bar area. They serve in order of numbers, so once my number came up, a waitress came over and took my order. The food came out very quickly, but we had no drinks. At which point I learned that you ordered from the bar area instead of with the waitress? A while after finishing my meal, I asked the person who took my food order for my bill since I had been waiting for it. She advised that I go to the bar and they cash me out. So I had to go back up to a very busy bar to have the bartenders cash me out. I'm exhausted just typing out the entire process again.


As far as the food goes, it was decent. The wings were unique, they're breaded like fried chicken, which was better than I had anticipated. It ended up being more pricey than I expected. I ordered 12 wings (50 cents each per the menu), fries, and a coke; my bill came out to $16. The math didn't add up, however, I felt awkward questioning it since the bartenders were slammed and trying to cash me out at the same time.


As decent as the food was, I don't anticipate I'll be coming back anytime soon. It was just too much of a cluster to go through the process, maybe if I had gotten a traditional table in the restaurant it would have been a little more smooth, but it was a mess all around.

Rating: 3.5/5
