The Green Bay Narrative

Episode 045 | Don Beebe

This week on The Green Bay Narrative, I'm joined by former Green Bay Packer and Super Bowl XXXI champion, Don Beebe. During my conversation with Don we talk about finally getting over the hump of winning a Super Bowl, what it was like playing for the Bills/Jaguars/Packers, Brett Favre, and so much more.

I hope you all enjoy this episode. It was a blast to record!

The episode was recorded prior to Mike McCarthy being fired, so some of the conversation is a little outdated now, but still a great episode regardless.
