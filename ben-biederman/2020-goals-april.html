My 2020 Goals - April Update

Time flies when you’re being quarantined! Unreal to think we nearly halfway through the year. It’s that time again to check in on how my goals are coming along for 2020.

I’m officially Reverend Biederman, at least that’s what I’ve been getting referred to in some emails by the place that ordained me. I’ve been enjoying the process a lot lately since it’s something I’m passionate about. Maybe next month I’ll debut it.

Status:

In Progress


As I reflect on April, I realize I didn’t get as much done as I would have liked to get done. To be honest though, I’m okay with it. With currently living through a quarantine, I’ve been trying to do things that keep me mentally sane. I’ve spent a lot of time working on my yard and doing projects around my house to help break up being inside. I’ve been also doing a lot of thinking about what I’d like to do with my life. I enjoy web development, but wonder if it’s something I’d ever be good enough to do full-time. It just seems to be one of those periods where you get stuck in your own head. For now, I’m going to just keep working towards goals and keep learning. Whatever is going to happen will happen, until then, I’m going to enjoy the ride. See you in May!
