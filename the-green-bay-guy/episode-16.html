The Green Bay Narrative

Episode 016 | Katie K.

During this episode of the Green Bay Narrative I sit down and speak with Katie. Katie is an awesome individual who despite her numerous flaws *cough* Starbucks and Pepsi *cough*, she has an immense love for the Green Bay Packers. Katie and I go back a few years, so the conversation with her was fun for me so that I could get to know her further. During the conversation we discuss her favorite Packers game, almost running into Brett Favre, how she almost became a Vikings fan, and so much more. A must listen.
