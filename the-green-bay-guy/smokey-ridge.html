Smokey Ridge

Located in a slightly hidden part of Green Bay resides Smokey Ridge. Smokey Ridge is a BBQ joint smokin' up deliciousness. Green Bay doesn't have a ton of BBQ options, with BBQ being one of my favorite foods, I'm always eager to try a new spot when I learn of one.

What to expect:

The first thing that caught my eye when we were being seated at Smokey Ridge was the view from the dining room. In the restaurant itself, the one wall is all windows. Outside the windows is a nice view of nature scenery. During our visit, it was the time of year where leaves are changing, so we were greeted by a beautiful view of fall.

After browsing their menu, I decided I wanted to try their pulled pork plate with corn muffins and side winders. After placing our order, we were given a complimentary basket of BBQ chips. It seemed like the chips were their own mixture of spice on them. It was a pleasant surprise and something nice to have while waiting for our food.

Our food was delivered quickly. It was immediately apparent that my eyes were bigger than my stomach. I knew immediately that there was no way I would eat all my food. Along with the food we received what may be best described as a bucket full of BBQ sauces. They make all their own sauces in house, which gave us some nice variety for our meal.

The food itself was great. Their pulled pork is marinated in a sweet honey glaze. The glaze blended nicely with the spices used on the pulled pork. Their corn muffins, which were made from scratch, made it the perfect BBQ meal to me.

All in all, Smokey Ridge may be my favorite BBQ spot in Green Bay at this time. If I'm craving BBQ, I'm definitely heading to Smokey Ridge. Great scenery, great food. Can't beat it.

Rating: 4.4/5
