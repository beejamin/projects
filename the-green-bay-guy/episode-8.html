The Green Bay Narrative

Episode 008 | Duke B.

Duke spends his Sundays preaching the good word, only to go home and say the good words (Go Pack Go!). On today's episode we learn about how Duke became a Packers fan despite growing up outside of Wisconsin, the Packers/Cowboys rivalry, and his first trip to Lambeau Field. Duke's positivity and great perspective is extremely refreshing. It was truly an honor to be able to sit down and speak with him on this episode. Sit back and enjoy Duke's stories in this episode of The Green Bay Narrative.
