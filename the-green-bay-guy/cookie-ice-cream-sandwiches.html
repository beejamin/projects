Cookie Ice Cream Sandwich

Cookies:
  • - 3 cups of all purpose flour
  • - 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • - 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • - 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • - 1 cup of room temperature butter, cut up
  • - 1 cup of brown sugar
  • - 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • - 2 large eggs
  • - 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • - 2 cups of chocolate chips or chunks
Misc.:
  • - Ice cream (Preference)
  • - Toppings of your choice to roll sandwich in

Directions:

Cookies:
  • 1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees and prep your baking sheets.
  • 2. In a bowl, combine flour, salt, baking powder, and baking soda. Set aside.
  • 3. In a separate bowl combine butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar.
  • 4. Using mixer, cream the butter and sugars together.
  • 5. Once the butter and sugars are creamed together, add eggs and vanilla. Cream together once again.
  • 6. Once creamed together, incorporate dry ingredient mixture into your creamed together mixture. Mix until fully creamed together.
  • 7. Mix in your chocolate chunks into the dough mixture with a spoon to not break down the chunks too much.
  • 8. Using cookie scoop, scoop equal sized cookie dough portions onto your baking sheets. Be sure to leave space for the cookie to expand.
  • 9. Bake for 8 - 10 minutes.
  • 10. Remove from oven after 8-10 minutes, allow to sit on baking sheet for additional 2 minutes.
  • 11. Transfer to cooling racks for 20-30 minutes.
Making the sandwich:
  • 1. Allow ice cream to sit out at room temperature for 5 minutes.
  • 2. Take two of your cookies that are even in size, put one face down. Place a scoop or two of ice cream on the upside down cookie. Add the other cookie on top of the ice cream. Using your palm, gently push down on the top cookie to make the sandwich.
  • 3. Roll the outside of the cookie sandwich in the toppings of your choice.
  • 4. Wrap ice cream cookie sandwich in plastic wrap.
  • 5. Place ice cream cookie sandwich in freezer, allow to sit for 1 - 2 hours.
  • 6. Enjoy
