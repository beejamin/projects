Bay Family Restaurant

The word family in Bay Family Restaurant really needs to be emphasized. I felt like I was at a restaurant owned by my family. I witnessed the staff speaking to individuals like it was a family reunion. Bay Family Restaurant projects a vibe of an old diner that serves up good comfort food.

What to expect:

The food that tastes much better than my terrible picture portrays it to be, Iâ€™ll start with that, haha. In addition to some good food, you have great prices with it. My girlfriend and I ate there and our bill was around $15, which I initially thought was an error since we also ordered cheese curds with our meal.


One thing to note about Bay Family Restaurant for those of you that are out of towners, they also offer one of the best places to stay while in Green Bay. The motel, much like the restaurant, isn't anything flashy but it gets the job at a great value. When you stay at the hotel, you get some vouchers towards the restaurant.


All in all, Bay Family Restaurant was a nice little spot to grab a bite to eat. I've said it a few times, it's nothing flashy, but it's a good family diner, and a good place to grab a good meal.

Rating: 4.0/5
