The Green Bay Narrative

Episode 050 | Ben B.

On this week's episode of The Green Bay Narrative, we're mixing it all up. Instead of me interviewing a guest, Wade (from episode #001) is interviewing me to hear my stories. During the conversation we talk about the podcast; the future of the website/content; how I became a Packers fan; my favorite player; and so much more. It's quite a long episode, it was originally intended to be a two-parter.


This episode is also available on video, click here to watch the video


I'd like to take an opportunity to thank all of you for your support over the past 50 episodes. Whether you've listened to one episode or all 50, I can't thank you enough. When I initially set out to do this podcast, I had no idea what to expect or how long it would go. I'm so grateful I had the opportunity to hear the stories I did and get to know the people I did from the podcast. While the podcast may be coming to an end for now, it's not the end of The Green Bay Guy. I'll continue to be updating the website and creating content.
