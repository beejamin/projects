Episode 037 | Roy Meulemans

What happens when you try and steal Vince Lombardi's hat after the Ice Bowl? Well, Roy Meulemans shares his experience on this week's episode of The Green Bay Narrative. After listening to the podcast, feel free to watch Roy tell his story through NFL Films.
