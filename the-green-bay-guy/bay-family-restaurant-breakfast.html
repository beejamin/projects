Bay Family Restaurant

Located off of Military Avenue in Green Bay, Wisconsin is Bay Motel & Family Restaurant. Positioned within walking distance of Lambeau Field, Bay Family Restaurant offers a variety of menu options for a great value.

What to expect:

The first thing you should expect when going to Bay Family Restaurant for breakfast is that you won't get anything flashy. Bay Family Restaurant is a small diner attached to their motel. Speaking of which, if you're looking for a place to stay when coming to a Packers game, my recommendation is Bay Motel. It's nothing flashy, much like their diner, however it's clean and affordable.


The food itself was very much a comfort meal, like I was eating at my parent's house. It was a skillet served with hash browns, bacons, sausage, cheese, and topped with some eggs. It wasn't anything too complex, given my palate I don't think many things I eat are, but it was a great breakfast dish that I'd definitely eat again.


All in all, Bay Family Restaurant was a nice spot to grab some breakfast but it wouldn't be my go-to place if I wanted to dine out and get some breakast. With so many great breakfast options in the area like The Pancake Place or Black Honey Hashery, I'd probably elect to go there instead. However, if you're staying at the Bay Motel, I'd definitely recommend going to the diner, especially because they give you some money off coupons to the diner. With that said, I'd definitely have breakfast there again, just maybe not my first choice.


Rating: 4.0/5
