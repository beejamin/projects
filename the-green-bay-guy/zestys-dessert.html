Zesty's

You scream, I scream, we all scream for ice cream!... Well, custard in this scenario. Zesty's has been serving up delicious custard for over 30 years. Zesty's offers rich custard served in a variety of ways. No better way to finish your night in Green Bay than to go grab some Zesty's custard and treat yourself.

What to expect:

Before going to Zesty's, make sure the location you're heading to is open. The Riverside location is only open during certain times of the year, generally closed in the winter months.


Zesty's is a fun joint to check out. It has that throwback vibe and is something I enjoy every summer. You can use the trail to walk alongside the river, all the way down to Zesty's on Riverside Drive. Then walk the trail a little bit afterwards to help burn off those calories. There's usually a line outside the building due to it being a stand and not a sit down joint. If you're looking to grab some custard on the go, there is a drive-thru however. The other Zesty's locations are year round restaurants where you can dine in or take it on the go with you.


If you're looking to grab a butter burger and some curds before getting dessert, they also offer some great food options as well. Check out Zesty's food review.


Another thing I love about Zesty's is their menu. They have some of my favorite custard dishes, as well as some great flavors of the day. If you're looking to satisfy your sweet tooth, I'd suggest giving Zesty's a go... just make sure the location you're going to is open before you head there!


Rating: 4.5/5
